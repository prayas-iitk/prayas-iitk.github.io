To Enable and To Inspire

Prayas is a complementary educational institution for individuals from underprivileged communities in and around IIT Kanpur

IIT Kanpur, and the neighbouring regions of Nankari, Barasirohi and Kalyanpur face fundamental limitations in school infrastructure, facilities and environment, particularly schools which cater to the underprivileged class. Prayas is an initiative born out of social responsibility. We complement schools and provide an alternative infrastructure for learning, growth and development of kids from such a class.

The team raises concerns over primary education, and the lack of access to schooling resources faced by this class. Our objective is to take these students to a level where they too can access the benefits of the current system of education.
